The Cafeteria

Cafeteria

Welcome to the Cafeteria, a pivotal spot in your adventure where choices can shape your journey! Here, you'll be presented with two questions which can have a detrimental effect on your adventure. They may seem easy and simple, but choose incorrectly and your escape plan may never materialise! Question one tests your courage and shows if you have what it takes to escape and remain calm in a tense scenario! Question two tests your decision making skills and will see if you choose to play it safe, or engage your inner stealth skills. Choose carefully, and enjoy the Cafeteria!
