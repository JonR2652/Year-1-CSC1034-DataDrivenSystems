Cafeteria

As you make your way through the cafeteria, suddenly one of the guards stops you, eyeing you with suspicion. "Hey, I have a question for you," he says, his voice stern. "Have you seen anything unusual around here? There's a food tray missing, and we're trying to figure out who might have taken it. Do you know anything about it?" He crosses his arms, waiting for your response. How will you respond? Choose wisely, as your decision could affect your fate.
