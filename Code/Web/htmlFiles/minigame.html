Rules and Regulations

  1. Avoid falling food: Dodge the food falling from
    above to stay in the game.
  2. You have 5 lives: Each time you get hit, you lose a life. Get hit 5 times, and it's game over!
  3. Movement controls: Use the left and right arrow keys to move your character and avoid the food.
  4. Time limit: You have a limited time to survive the food fight. Stay alert!
  5. Objective: Survive as long as you can and avoid getting hit by the falling food!
